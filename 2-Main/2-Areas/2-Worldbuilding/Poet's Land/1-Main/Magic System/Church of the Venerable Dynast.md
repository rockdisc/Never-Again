#Worldbuilding/Factions, #Worldbuilding/PoetsLand/Gods 

## Summary 

#### God of worship: [[Venerable Dynast]]
Mainly located in [[Ciaodan]] the Land of Thousand Rivers. Though they are gaining popularity in other regions like [[Presford]].

## Beliefs

There are a court of gods and goddesses called Divas who work under the [[Venerable Dynast]]. It is his dynasty. He rules over the rivers and skies. 

> [!important] 
> They consider the sky and sea the same thing with several people going out on adventures and tales about people finding a way to get to the sky by going to the end of the sea. 

Though Western populations may make fun of it, there is no proof to say anything but. 

Though with the larger Eastern(Western) influence, there is a growing number of people who are representing the [[Venerable Dynast]] as a woman. This is causing them to change between genders. 

They pray by cupping their hands in front of their chest as though they are trying to catch rain.

## Thousand-High Guard 

Each of them are specifically picked as the strongest and most devout believers by the [[Venerable Dynast]] himself. They gain the power of [[Ikon]]s. An individual can be used to clear out a city. 

## Hidden Truths 

There are multiple [[Ikon]]s who became gods who are also [[Transmigrators]]. Due to this reason there are a lot of Eastern mixes here.
