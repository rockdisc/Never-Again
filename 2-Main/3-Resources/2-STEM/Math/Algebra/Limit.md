#Math/Algebra 

It is used to see what [[Polynomials]] will do when reaching toward [[Infinite]]. This is for [[Domain]] and [[Range]]. It is notated as $\lim_{ x \to \infty }$.

$\lim_{ x \to -\infty }$ is the negative side, $\lim_{ x \to \infty }$ for right. 
$$
\lim_{ x \to \infty } f(x)=\infty
$$
means as $x$ approaches $\infty$, $f(x)$ approaches $\infty$. 

## End behavior 

The degree and coefficient of [[Polynomials]] affects the end behavior. 

| Degree | Coefficient | End Behavior                                                                                       |
| ------ | ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Odd    | Positive    | /, like $y=x$,<br>$\lim_{ x \to - \infty }f(x)=-\infty$ <br>$\lim_{ x \to \infty }f(x)= \infty$    |
| Odd    | Negative    | \. like $y=-x$<br>$\lim_{ x \to - \infty }f(x)=\infty$ <br>$\lim_{ x \to \infty }f(x)= -\infty$    |
| Even   | Positive    | U, like $y=x^2$<br>$\lim_{ x \to - \infty }f(x)=\infty$ <br>$\lim_{ x \to \infty }f(x)= \infty$    |
| Even   | Negative    | n, like $y=-x^2$<br>$\lim_{ x \to - \infty }f(x)=-\infty$ <br>$\lim_{ x \to \infty }f(x)=- \infty$ |
