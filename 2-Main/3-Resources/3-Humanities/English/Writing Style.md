# Diction 

The writer's word choice. This effects how the words impact the reader. **Denotation** is the word's dictionary definition while **connotation** is the feeling of the word. Diction focuses on both. There is formal diction, which stick to traditional grammar rules and prose. And informal diction which explore more ways of writing language.
