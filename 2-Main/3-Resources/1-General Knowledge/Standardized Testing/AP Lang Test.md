#School/StandardizedTesting 

## Essay Writing 

It is graded on a 1-6 scale. There are 3 categories whose totals add up to 6. 
The first one is [[Thesis]] with 1 point, the want it to respond to the prompt and be defensible. It can be anywhere in the text, but is most often in the start. 
Then there is [[CER]], it is pretty self-explanatory. It is out of 4 points. It should have correctly cited evidence and good commentary which explains and expands on the text. 
Last is sophistication with 1 point, it is just if the person shows understanding of the text and is sort of a unicorn point.
